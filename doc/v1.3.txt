 Features
 --------
 - Upload and download
   - Works in the background even if the app is killed
   - Retries with exponential hold off if the server is not reachable.
   (you can test by sending videos in airplane mode)

 - Slide your finger down when recording to abort. Recording is discarded.
 
 - Video Status
   - A temporary text indicator in the name text field indicates the status of the message
     - p...   Still on your phone being uploaded
     - rn...  Still on your phone retrying for the nth time
     - .s..   On the server 
     - ..p.   On the recipients phone
     - ...v!  Viewed!
